More About Motivated Mindset Dancers Studio

Our Mission

We help not because we're rich, but because we give from the little we have. If you have, it doesn't mean everyone has. Many people need your small help, and your contribution can make a big difference in supporting orphans and needy children at Motivated Mindset Dancers Studio.

We support children and walk their journey with them, not because we have, but because of the challenging path they have walked. If you have been through tough times, you understand what these challenges can put young children through.

Our mission is to ensure no child is left out of school, lacking resources or opportunities. Education is key to ending household poverty, and we continue walking this bumpy road until these amazing children shape their future through quality education.

Supporting Single Mothers

Inspired by online challenges and judgments, we started a sisterhood for single mothers — a safe space for conversations, support, and empowerment. Weekly meetups, love, laughter, and shared ideas help them raise their children single-handedly.

Not all single mothers come with drama. Many are educated, independent, and raising the next generation of leaders. They're defined by their strength, resilience, and determination. They deserve respect and encouragement for their efforts.

Unique Programs: Camping & Life Skills

MMDS is the only dance studio in Mathare slums that organizes camping experiences for children. They gain more than dance skills — mental health awareness, mentorship, leadership, creative training, and life skills.

Despite social media challenges, our mission is bigger than numbers — it’s about changing lives. We are calling for partnerships, collaborations, and donations to keep this safe, empowering space alive.

Mental Health Advocacy

We advocate for mental health, fighting suicide thoughts and supporting children in crisis. Many learn to cope alone, but our mission is to provide guidance, resilience, and hope.

Healing changes how children let people in — your support ensures no child is left unheard or unseen. May our dancers never die of depression.

Spiritual & Community Values

We partner with local churches to instill Christian values in children. Our holistic programs promote spiritual growth, healthy social interactions, and physical wellness — shaping confident and purpose-driven young individuals.

Menstrual Hygiene & Pad Bank

Menstrual hygiene is not a side issue. It is dignity. It is confidence. It is protection. At Motivated Mindset Dancers Studio, we refuse to stay silent about menstrual health — because as long as we have young teenage girls, period shame has no place here.

Too many girls miss practice, lose confidence, or feel embarrassed simply because they lack access to sanitary pads. We choose to protect them instead. We choose to talk. We choose to act.

That is why we created a Pad Bank — a safe space where anyone can donate pads, and where our dancers can access them freely, without shame or fear. This initiative has helped our girls stay committed, confident, and fully present in the studio and in school.

To support our dancers throughout the year, we require at least 1,000 boxes of sanitary pads annually. Sponsorships allow us to plan sustainably, reach every girl consistently, and expand menstrual health education alongside pad distribution.

Your support is more than a donation. It is reassurance. It is confidence. It is love in action. Partner with us. Sponsor our Pad Bank. Help us end period shame — one girl at a time.

Donations help us sustain dance training, mentorship programs, environmental clean-ups, menstrual hygiene support, and community outreach. Funding allows us to plan long-term and reach more children consistently.

You can sponsor:

3. Donate Materials

Support us by donating:

Organizations, NGOs, and companies can partner with us through CSR programs, joint projects, or long-term collaborations that empower youth through arts and community development.

5. Volunteer Your Skills

We welcome volunteers who can offer:

6. Spread the Word

Share our story, invite others to donate, and help amplify the voices of young dancers in Huruma Slum. Every contribution — big or small — helps a child stay confident, disciplined, and hopeful.

Support Motivated Mindset Dancers Studio. Invest in talent. Restore dignity. Build futures.
