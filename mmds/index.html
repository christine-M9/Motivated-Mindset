About Us

Motivated Mindset Dancers Studio is a community-based organization based in Mathare, Nairobi, dedicated to empowering children and youth through dance and holistic development. Founded five years ago, the studio is the only dance facility in Mathare slums, serving vulnerable children from informal settlements.

We have positively impacted over 2,000 children, offering them a safe space to learn, grow, and dream beyond their circumstances. Currently, we support 42 children, including orphans and those raised by single parents, helping them access education, mentorship, life skills, and artistic training.

Vision

To be a leading community-based dance and creative arts hub that transforms the lives of children and youth from marginalized communities.

Mission

To empower children and youth from informal settlements through dance by providing safe spaces, quality training, mentorship, and life skills.
